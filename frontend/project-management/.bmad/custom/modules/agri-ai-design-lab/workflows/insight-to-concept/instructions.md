# Insight to Concept Instructions

<workflow>
  <step n="1" goal="Gather inputs">
    <action>Collect synthesized insights, personas, field notes, and data summaries.</action>
    <action>Identify current pain points, opportunities, and unmet needs.</action>
  </step>
  <step n="2" goal="Frame opportunity areas">
    <action>Group insights into themes (product, service, operations).</action>
    <action>Use "How might we" prompts to articulate opportunities.</action>
  </step>
  <step n="3" goal="Ideate AI concepts">
    <action>Generate AI-enabled concepts for each opportunity including user benefit, AI role, data needs.</action>
    <action>List enabling technologies (chatbots, decision support, automation, vision, etc.).</action>
  </step>
  <step n="4" goal="Score feasibility & impact">
    <action>Score each concept on impact, feasibility, differentiation, speed.</action>
    <action>Flag ethical, regulatory, or adoption considerations.</action>
  </step>
  <step n="5" goal="Prioritize concepts">
    <action>Rank shortlist (top 3) with rationale.</action>
    <action>Recommend next experiments or prototypes.</action>
  </step>
  <step n="6" goal="Document concept sheet">
    <action>Populate template and save to {output_folder}.</action>
  </step>
</workflow>
