# Impact Pitch Kit Instructions

<workflow>
  <step n="1" goal="Clarify audience">
    <action>Identify whether the pitch targets investors, donors, or enterprise clients.</action>
    <action>Capture decision criteria and preferred language.</action>
  </step>
  <step n="2" goal="Assemble narrative">
    <action>Summarize problem, solution, differentiation, and traction.</action>
    <action>Link to SDG goals and localized impact metrics.</action>
  </step>
  <step n="3" goal="Highlight product evidence">
    <action>Embed UX/UI previews, AI features, prototype links.</action>
    <action>List roadmap milestones and delivery confidence.</action>
  </step>
  <step n="4" goal="Define financials & KPIs">
    <action>Provide business model summary, cost/revenue assumptions, and KPI targets.</action>
  </step>
  <step n="5" goal="Output pitch kit">
    <action>Populate template, save to {output_folder}/pitches, and outline presentation next steps.</action>
  </step>
</workflow>
