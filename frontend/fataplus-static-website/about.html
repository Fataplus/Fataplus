About Fataplus

Pioneering Agritech Design in Africa, Transforming Agriculture Through Innovation

Our Story

Founded in 2023 by Fenohery Fanomezanirina, Fataplus emerged from a vision to transform African agriculture through innovative design and technology. As Madagascar's first product design agency specializing in agritech, we recognized the critical need for digital solutions tailored to the unique challenges faced by African farmers.

Our journey began with a simple yet powerful question: How can we leverage design thinking and technology to empower farmers, increase agricultural productivity, and contribute to food security across the African continent?

Today, Fataplus stands as a beacon of innovation in Madagascar's tech ecosystem, bridging the gap between traditional agricultural practices and cutting-edge digital solutions.

Founded 2023

Our Mission

To empower African farmers and agricultural communities through innovative digital design solutions that increase productivity, improve livelihoods, and promote sustainable farming practices.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading agritech design agency, creating transformative digital solutions that make Africa a global hub for agricultural innovation and technology.
