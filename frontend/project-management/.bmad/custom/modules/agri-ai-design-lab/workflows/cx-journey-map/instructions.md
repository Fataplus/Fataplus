# CX Journey Map Instructions

<workflow>
  <step n="1" goal="Select persona & lifecycle">
    <action>Determine which persona and lifecycle stage(s) to map.</action>
  </step>
  <step n="2" goal="Document stages & touchpoints">
    <action>List awareness, onboarding, activation, retention, and advocacy stages.</action>
    <action>Capture user actions, emotions, and CX metrics for each stage.</action>
  </step>
  <step n="3" goal="Link delivery operations">
    <action>Identify supporting teams, processes, and tools.</action>
    <action>Note enablement materials and training needs.</action>
  </step>
  <step n="4" goal="Highlight improvements">
    <action>Spot friction points and design opportunities.</action>
    <action>Recommend experiments or playbooks to improve CX/DX.</action>
  </step>
  <step n="5" goal="Output journey map">
    <action>Populate template, save under {output_folder}/journeys, share summary.</action>
  </step>
</workflow>
