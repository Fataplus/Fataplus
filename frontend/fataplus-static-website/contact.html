Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with Fataplus

What makes Fataplus different from other design agencies?

+

We're Madagascar's first agritech-specialized product design agency. Our unique understanding of African agricultural challenges, combined with our technical expertise and local context, allows us to create solutions that truly work for farmers and agricultural businesses.

Do you work with international clients?

+

Absolutely! We work with clients globally who are interested in African agricultural markets or who need our specialized agritech design expertise. Our team is fluent in English, French, and Malagasy.

What is your typical project timeline?

+

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. A simple UX/UI design project might take 4-6 weeks, while a full product development could take 3-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you offer ongoing support after project completion?

+

Yes, we offer various support packages including maintenance, updates, and continued optimization. We believe in building long-term partnerships with our clients.
