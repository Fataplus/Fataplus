# FataPlus Documentation

Welcome to the official documentation for FataPlus, an agricultural marketplace platform designed to connect farmers, sellers, and buyers in Madagascar.

## What is FataPlus?

FataPlus is a comprehensive platform that enables:

- **Farmers** to showcase and sell their products directly to consumers
- **Sellers** to find and purchase agricultural products at fair prices
- **Consumers** to access fresh, locally-grown produce
- **Community members** to share knowledge and best practices

## Key Features

- **Marketplace**: Buy and sell agricultural products
- **Community**: Connect with other farmers and share knowledge
- **Learning Resources**: Access educational content about farming techniques
- **Real-time Updates**: Get notifications about new products, orders, and community posts
- **Offline Support**: Use the app even without an internet connection (PWA)

## Tech Stack

FataPlus is built with modern technologies:

- **Frontend**: React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Shadcn UI
- **Backend**: PocketBase (replacing Supabase)
- **Real-time**: WebSockets for live updates
- **Deployment**: Vercel with Turborepo
- **PWA**: Service workers for offline functionality
