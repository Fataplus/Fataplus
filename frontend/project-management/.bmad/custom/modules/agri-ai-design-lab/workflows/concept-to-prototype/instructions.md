# Concept to Prototype Instructions

<workflow>
  <step n="1" goal="Select concept">
    <action>Confirm which concept from the shortlist is moving forward.</action>
    <action>Review target personas, key jobs-to-be-done, and success criteria.</action>
  </step>
  <step n="2" goal="Define experience architecture">
    <action>Create a user journey or service blueprint summarizing touchpoints.</action>
    <action>Outline primary and secondary workflows within the product.</action>
  </step>
  <step n="3" goal="Design UX/UI foundations">
    <action>Draft IA & sitemap, key screens, component requirements.</action>
    <action>Reference Fataplus design system tokens and accessibility guidelines.</action>
  </step>
  <step n="4" goal="Plan no-code / AI build">
    <action>Identify target platform (Bubble, FlutterFlow, Glide, custom).</action>
    <action>Break down features into sprints, specify data models, APIs, automations.</action>
  </step>
  <step n="5" goal="Prototype plan output">
    <action>Summarize deliverables, owners, and timeline.</action>
    <action>List validation checkpoints and analytics instrumentation.</action>
    <action>Populate template and save to {output_folder}.</action>
  </step>
</workflow>
